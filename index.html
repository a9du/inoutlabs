We are in the Riverbank Shopping Center on Dempster St. in Morton Grove. 1 mile west of I-94 and 3 miles east of I-294. Click here for directions.

Via Public Transportation
Red Line to Howard. Transfer to Yellow Line (Skokie Swift) to Dempster St. PACE 250 Bus west to Lehigh. 2 min walk.

or

Metra: Milwaukee Road (toward Fox Lake) to Dempster St stop. 9 minute walk.

Business Hours

All times Central
Monday to Friday: 8:30 AM – 4:00 PM

If you are visiting our clinic, please arrive at least 45 min. before close.

NO URINE DRUG SCREENS AFTER 3:00 PM MON-FRI

We are closed the following holidays:

New Year’s Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day (July 4), Labor Day, Thanksgiving,
Black Friday, Christmas Eve and Christmas Day.

For emergency and after-hours drug and alcohol testing call 847-657-7900 and select option 2.
